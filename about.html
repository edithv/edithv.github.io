about me

my major

My name is Edith and I am a junior at Cornell University, majoring in Computer Science with an external specialization in Operations Research (ORIE) and Information Science, Systems, and Technology (ISST).

My interests lie in software development and product design. On campus, I am the co-treasurer for the Association of Computer Science Undergraduates (ACSU) and an economic analyst for Design by Biomedical Undergraduates Engineering Project Team (DEBUT).
